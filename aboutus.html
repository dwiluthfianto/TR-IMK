ABOUT

The Culture

The Culture was a way to showcase the creativity and diversity of the employees. Each department was assigned a theme and a medium, and had to collaborate on a piece of art that represented their work and values. The Culture project not only enchanced the aesthetic appeal of the office, but also fostered a sense of pride and belonging among the staff
